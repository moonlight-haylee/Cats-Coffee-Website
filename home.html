The First Cat Cafe in the City!

Welcome to Las Cruces Cats & Coffee, where our mission is to provide a relaxing environment to enjoy your meal, snack, and/or drink and get the chance to meet and play with the cats we have available for adoption! We work closely with animal shelters in the area who need extra support in giving these lovely companions a loving home, or some extra love during the day while they wait!

All of the costs of treats, playtime, merchandise, and any other fees will be allocated to the needs of the cats, such as food, toys, vet bills, etc.
